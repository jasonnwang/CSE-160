Please use a browser that supports "canvas"

Controls:

Movement:

  • W - Move Forward
  • S - Move Backward
  • A - Move Left
  • D - Move Right
  • Q - Pan Left
  • E - Pan Right

Block Actions:

  • P - Place a Block
  • O - Remove a Block

Pointer Lock:

  • Click the canvas to engage mouse control (pointer lock).
  • Press "ESC" to release the mouse.
